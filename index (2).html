School Achievements

#1 High School in the Adams 12 school district and #3 in Colorado - 2021 US News and World Report
Stargate Charter School is ranked fifth within Colorado. Students have the opportunity to take Advanced Placement® coursework and exams. The AP® participation rate at Stargate Charter School is 83%. The total minority enrollment is 42%. Stargate Charter School is 1 of 11 high schools in the Adams 12 Five Star Schools.


#1 Elementary School, #1 Middle School, #1 High School and #1 Best Public High School Teachers - 2021-2022 Niche rankings
We believe every student deserves the best learning environment possible. At Stargate, we’ve spent more than 25 years building a nurturing and thoughtful educational community for intellectually gifted learners and their families. From kindergarten through twelfth grade, we work to challenge each student’s academic abilities, support their unique emotional needs, promote individual character development and encourage a life-long love of learning. Our students are taught to think critically, ask questions and view mistakes as an important part of the learning process.
